outer loop

Marine Predators Algorithm

outer loopSharkMPA

Lévy flight ocean hunting

How It Works

Simulates three foraging phases based on velocity ratio: (1) High velocity – Brownian exploration, (2) Medium velocity – Lévy transition, (3) Low velocity – Lévy exploitation. Fish Aggregating Devices (FADs) add random perturbation.

Key Equations

Phase 1 — Brownian Exploration

When prey moves faster than predator (t < T/3), agents explore via Brownian random motion B around the prey matrix P.

Phase 2 — Lévy Transition

Mid-phase (T/3 < t < 2T/3) uses Lévy flight L to balance exploration and exploitation via heavy-tailed jumps.

FADs Effect

Fish Aggregating Devices perturb agents with probability fads = 0.2 to simulate natural environmental disturbance.
