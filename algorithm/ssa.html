Salp Swarm Algorithm

inner loopSalpSSA

Chain-linked ocean drifting

How It Works

Salps form a chain: the leader (index 0) moves toward the food source with decreasing parameter c₁, while followers update via Newton’s law: pos = 0.5·(pos[i] + pos[i−1]). Food tracks the best solution found.

Key Equations

Exploration Coefficient

c₁ decreases exponentially, shrinking the leader salp’s movement range from exploration to exploitation.

Leader Salp Update

The leader salp moves toward or away from the food source F. Direction is chosen randomly (c₃ < 0.5).

Follower Update (Newton’s Law)

Follower salps average their position with the predecessor, forming a chain-linked movement pattern.

Parameters

pop_size20int · 5 – 200

Number of salps in the chain

Number of SSA iterations
