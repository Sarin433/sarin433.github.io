outer loop

Ant Colony Optimization

outer loopAntACO

Pheromone trail foraging

How It Works

Ants build tours via probabilistic selection based on pheromone trails and heuristic distance. After each iteration, pheromones evaporate and successful trails are reinforced.

Key Equations

Node Selection Probability

Ant k at node i selects next node j based on pheromone intensity τ and heuristic η = 1/distance. α and β control the relative importance of trail vs. proximity.

Pheromone Evaporation

After each iteration, pheromone trails decay by factor ρ (evaporation rate) to forget suboptimal paths.

Pheromone Deposit

Each ant k deposits pheromone proportional to Q/Lk, where Lk is its tour distance. Shorter tours leave stronger trails.
