outer loop

Genetic Algorithm

outer loopDNA CreatureGA

Natural selection & mutation

How It Works

Evolves a population of permutations through tournament selection, OX1 crossover for recombination, and swap mutation. Elitism preserves the best individual each generation.

Key Equations

Tournament Selection

Select the fittest individual from a random tournament of size 3 as a parent for crossover.

Swap Mutation

Randomly swap two positions in the tour sequence with mutation probability pm = 0.2 to maintain diversity.

Parameters

pop_size15int · 2 – 100

Population size per generation

generations10int · 1 – 500

Number of evolutionary generations
